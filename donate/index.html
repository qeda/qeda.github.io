Donate

QEDA is a non-commercial project. It exists thanks to the efforts of enthusiasts.

If you have the success story of using QEDA you could make your own contribution to the project. Any donation would be much appreciated.

Supporter

Those who can afford to donate $10 per month receive special Supporter status. They may ask to prioritize their issues or library requests on GitHub. Also they can be added to Supporters List below by request. Please feel free to write us to donate@qeda.org for additional information.
